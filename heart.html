❤️ Heart Disease Awareness

What is Heart Disease?

  • ❤️Heart disease is a general term used to describe conditions that affect the structure and function of the heart.
  • The heart is an important organ that pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients to tissues and organs. When the heart is affected by disease, it may not pump blood efficiently, which can affect the normal functioning of the body.
  • Heart disease can involve problems with the heart muscle, blood vessels, valves, or the electrical system that controls the heartbeat.
  • These problems may develop slowly over a long period of time and can be caused by factors such as aging, lifestyle, infections, or genetic conditions.
    • Heart disease is one of the most common health problems affecting adults around the world.

Causes

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Obesity
  • Diabetes

Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Irregular heartbeat

Prevention

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid smoking
  • Manage stress
  • Regular medical check-ups
